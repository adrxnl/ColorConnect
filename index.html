About Color Connect

ColorConnect is designed to support individuals with color vision deficiency by helping them recognize colors accurately in their daily lives. The app begins with the Ishihara Color Blindness Test to identify the user's specific type of color blindness. Based on the results, it applies real-time color correction filters tailored to their needs. Beyond correction, ColorConnect makes visual exploration more engaging through features like the Art Timeline and Art Explorer, where users can interact with curated artworks adapted to their vision. Users can also view and manage their saved images through a personal Gallery. With a user-friendly and accessible interface, ColorConnect ensures an intuitive and inclusive experience for everyone.
